About Us

Our firm MARCONI INDIA SOLAR INFOTECH, has began its journey in February 2022 as an Electro Mechanical Consultant, Engineers & Contractors at Jalgaon(MH). It made a humble beginning from Maintenance of EHV transmission line works. Now with the spread of work, Maintenance of EHV lines and Substations, Industrial / Corporate offices Electrification, Design, Supply & Erection and commissioning of EHV/HT/LT System, Designing of Control / Power Panel, and allied Electrical Works is being done.
